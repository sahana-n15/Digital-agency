costs of goods sold (COGS) refers to the expenses incurred by a creative digital agency in delivering its services to clients. This can include costs related to human resources, such as salaries and benefits for employees involved in service delivery, as well as overhead costs such as software licenses, equipment, and office space. These costs are directly attributed to the production and delivery of services and are subtracted from the agency's revenue to calculate its gross profit.
